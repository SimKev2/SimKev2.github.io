Projects

LED Music Visualizer

In my second semester here I took CprE 186, a course designed to allow students to create a project on their own. I worked in a group of 5 people including myself. We chose to create a music visualizer, our idea being that you could input a .mp3 file and the waveform would show up on our custom LED board, much like the one seen here, in real time. My main part of the project involved setting up our git repo and then much of the actual soldering and wiring of the board. I was able to learn a lot about practical building of hobby level hardware, and could now confidently build a small project on my own even if it involves electronics. We made heavy use of our schools Electronic Technology Group for both guidance and hardware.
