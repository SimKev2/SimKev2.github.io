How we met

We met back at Linn-Mar High School at a football game in 2013. Our first unofficial date was going to Perkins after that game. We then started hanging out and eventually went to our first school dance: Homecoming 2013. Shortly after this on October 20th, 2013, we started officially dating. Over the past six years, we have continued to grow together. Both attending and graduating from Iowa State University, we now live in Ankeny, Iowa, and got engaged on October 19th, 2019.

Our new date

Life really is full of surprises. Shortly after mailing our Save the Dates, we learned that our previous event venue went bankrupt and cancelled all event bookings. While this was a shock, it was early enough in our planning that we can thankfully reschedule. With that, we have booked a new location! Our new date is in 2021, on Saturday, April 24th. We will be sending out some more mail with a sticker to update any save the dates. We hope everyone can still make it!
